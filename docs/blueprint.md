# **App Name**: Powerlift Pro

## Core Features:

- Multi-User Role-Based Access: Athletes can track their progress, while coaches have full management capabilities over multiple athlete profiles, including creation, modification, and deletion.
- Dynamic Training Plan Management: Create and manage multi-week training blocks with personalized exercises, sets, reps, and calculated weights based on individual 1RM percentages for Squat, Bench, and Deadlift.
- Comprehensive Exercise & Mobility Library: Access a rich database of powerlifting and accessory exercises with integrated tutorial links. Coaches can also add and manage custom exercises and structured mobility routines for athletes.
- Personalized Performance Dashboard: Visualize athlete progress through interactive charts tracking 1RM evolution for core lifts (Squat, Bench, Deadlift), weekly training volume, and intensity trends over various time periods (week, month, year).
- AI Training Optimization Tool: Leverage a generative AI tool to suggest intelligent adjustments to training programs or recommend supplementary exercises, based on historical performance data and an athlete's progression patterns, to optimize performance.
- Coach-Specific Data Management & Controls: Empower coaches with tools to import/export the entire athlete database, delete athlete records, reset progress, and efficiently duplicate or remove training weeks for streamlined program administration.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary interactive color: Energetic Gold (#EAB308), reflecting achievement and power, used for calls-to-action, active states, and highlights throughout the dark interface.
- Accent color: Assertive Red (#DC2626), utilized for important alerts, coach-specific actions, and to signify intensity, creating a balanced visual contrast with the primary gold.
- Background color: Deep charcoal black (#000000), providing a high-contrast canvas for content and contributing to a serious, performance-oriented atmosphere with subtle darker gradients for depth.
- Secondary and text colors: A range of dark to light grays (e.g., text-gray-200, gray-500, gray-900) ensure readability and enhance the modern, minimalistic aesthetic.
- Headlines and prominent text: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), a robust and tech-inspired font that conveys strength and modernity, ideal for titles and emphasis.
- Body and descriptive text: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its excellent readability across different screen sizes and its neutral, objective presentation of numerical and explanatory content.
- Crisp and clear line icons from the 'Lucide' library, in black, gold, or red, effectively supporting visual metaphors for fitness activities, data analysis, and coaching actions.
- A dark, immersive UI with a strong grid system and flexible components, ensuring full responsiveness across all device sizes with a clear hierarchical structure for optimal user engagement.
- Subtle, quick transitions on interactive elements (e.g., button presses, tab switches) and smooth fade-in effects for content changes, enhancing user feedback and interface dynamism.
